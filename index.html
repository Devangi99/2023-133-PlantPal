About PlantPal

Growth Monitoring

Our Growth Monitoring & Predictions component is all about nurturing plants with precision. Using React Native and Flask, we've created a cross-platform marvel. We analyze user-uploaded images and data like planting date and dominant branch visibility to predict your plant's growth stage. By factoring in environmental conditions and fertilizer details, we can even forecast when your plant will reach its industry-expected level. With attractive UIs and enhanced UX, your dreams take root.

Leaf Affliction Analysis

With the power of React Native and Flask, our Leaf Affliction Analysis & Shipment Recommendations component revolutionizes plant care. Snap a leaf image, and our app precisely measures affected areas, distinguishing diseases and nutrient imbalances like powdery mildew and NPK deficiencies. Your plant's vitality is paramount, as we provide data-driven advice for optimal growth and health. Your trusted partner in plant well-being.

Plant Guide

Experience plant variety discovery like never before with our Plant Recommendation & Variety Identification component. Empowered by React Native and Flask, our app effortlessly identifies Philodendron plant varieties from images. But it doesn't end there; we assist growers in making informed decisions on cut flower crops based on local weather, resources, and yield data. Our intuitive interface with attractive UIs and enhanced UX caters to plant enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Demand Prediction

Stay ahead in the ornamental plant industry with our Demand Prediction for Ornamental Plants. Utilizing IoT sensor data for humidity, soil moisture, and temperature, our solution forecasts demand for various plant types and pot sizes. Powered by React Native and Flask, our technology delivers data-backed insights to simplify the future of your plant business. It's the intelligent way to anticipate market trends and optimize your offerings.
